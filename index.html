A Coalition for Artistic Freedom
Across Cultures

We bring together performing arts organizations — Asian, Indigenous, Western classical, Modern Jazz, and beyond — to protect, amplify, and advance artistic expression in all its forms.

Who We Are

An Independent Voice for the Arts

Lake of Sunrise is a non-governmental organization uniting performing arts groups across cultural traditions. We exist because artistic communities deserve a shared platform — one that speaks across institutional boundaries and geographic borders.


Our members include organizations rooted in Asian performing arts traditions, Indigenous cultural practices, Western classical music and theater, Modern Jazz, and contemporary interdisciplinary work. What unites us is a belief that artistic freedom is not a privilege — it is a right.

"Art does not belong to a single tradition. Freedom in artistic expression is a universal value, and advocating for it requires a coalition that reflects that universality."

What We Do

Advocacy Through Community and Communication

We empower member organizations to reach audiences and decision-makers directly — providing tools, platforms, and collective support to ensure their voices are heard.

🤝

Coalition Building

We connect arts organizations across cultural traditions, creating a network where shared challenges become collective opportunities and where diverse voices strengthen one another.

📢

Direct Outreach

We provide members with tools to communicate directly with their audiences, patrons, and policymakers — without intermediaries filtering or limiting their message.

🛡️

Principled Advocacy

We advocate for policies and environments that protect artistic freedom — at institutional, governmental, and community levels — grounded in respect for cultural diversity.

Our Commitment

Privacy by Principle

We believe that the tools we provide to members should be trustworthy, transparent, and respectful of everyone they touch — including artists, audiences, and email recipients.

Lake of Sunrise does not collect, store, or sell any personal data of artists, audiences, or email recipients. All tools provided to members operate solely through members' own accounts.
